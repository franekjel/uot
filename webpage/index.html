Universe Of Technology

Humans finally discovered the technology of intergalactic travel... It turned out to be very difficult, so largest nations in the Milky Way have joined forces and together decided to send an expedition to a newly found galaxy. They opened a wormhole together and each country sent one crew. Due to energy shortage, after a while the tunnel closed. Due to lack of energy the crews were scattered across the galaxy and lost contact. It has been discovered that the galaxy was once inhabited by a very advanced civilization, but for unknown reasons, it became extinct. Only relics remained. There are countless resources and technologies in the galaxy that humans have never dreamed of. For obvious reasons, each country wants to take over the galaxy just for itself.

Build, colonize and fight. Take over enemy planets and use the resources of asteroids. Upgrade ships, discover new technologies. Become the ruler of the galaxy!

Only the strongest will survive... Join the game and fight for your galactic empire!!!
