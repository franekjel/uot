Universe Of Technology

The game was implemented as an university project of six students at the Warsaw University of Technology.

The goal of the project was to deliver a 4X online video game. This work focuses on allowing a simultaneous gameplay for many players on one server. The main programming language is C++. Visualization and sound are created via SDL library.

Users can create their own servers and join them to play together. The goal of the game is to eliminate other players' fleets and take over their territories. To create fleets, you must have large amounts of resources that are obtained from colonized planets and asteroids, as well as from stars.

Only the strongest will survive... Join them and become a part of the intergalactic empire!!!
